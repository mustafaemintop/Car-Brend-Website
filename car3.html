The data was determined using measurement methods required by law. Since 1 September 2017, new vehicles have been subject to type approval according to the Worldwide Harmonized Light Vehicles Test Procedure (WLTP), a more realistic test procedure for measuring fuel consumption and CO2 emissions. From 1 September 2018, WLTP will replace the New European Driving Cycle (NEDC). Due to the more realistic test conditions, fuel consumption and CO2 emission values ​​measured according to WLTP are in many cases higher than those measured under NEDC. This may result in changes to vehicle taxation from 1 September 2018.
